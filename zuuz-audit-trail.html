FAQ

Common questions.

What is captured in the A2A audit log?+
Every action taken by an A2A agent is logged: the triggering event, the classification decision, the resolution steps taken, the outcome, and the timestamp. Logs are immutable — they cannot be edited or deleted by any user, including administrators.
How long are audit logs retained?+
Audit log retention is configurable by enterprise plan. Default retention is 7 years to align with common regulatory requirements. Custom retention periods are available on Enterprise plans.
Can logs be exported for external compliance review?+
Yes. Logs can be exported in JSON or CSV format from the admin console or via API. Exports can be filtered by date range, actor, action type, and system. Export events are themselves logged.
Is the Legal Hold audit trail admissible in legal proceedings?+
The Legal Hold log is a cryptographically signed, timestamped record of every access attempt and every hold status change. We recommend consulting your legal counsel on admissibility in your specific jurisdiction.
