Pricing FAQ

Pricing questions.

Everything about the model — answered.

What is a Work Credit?+
A Work Credit is the unit of consumption for ZUUZ agents. Each task type costs a fixed number of credits — from 1 credit for an email read to 20 credits for a CEO Approval Cockpit. Credits are only charged when a task completes successfully. Failed or incomplete tasks cost nothing.
Can credits be shared across departments?+
Yes. Work Credits are universal across all active departments. If Legal has a quiet month, the credits carry over to HR automatically. There are no siloed budgets per use case or per department.
What happens when we run out of credits?+
Agents queue rather than fail. You're notified when you reach 80% usage. Top-up packs can be purchased at any time from the admin console. Enterprise plans include a credit overage buffer.
Is there really no per-seat pricing?+
Correct. Every employee in your organization gets access to ZUUZ. Role assignment determines what they see — the CEO sees the Approval Cockpit, the paralegal sees the contract workflow. Neither costs more because there are more people.
What's included in the 14-day trial?+
One department module of your choice, 100 Work Credits, full platform access, and live connections to your existing stack. No credit card. If you connect 3 systems and run 50 tasks and decide it's not for you, nothing is charged.
How is the Enterprise plan different?+
Enterprise engagements include custom platform terms, custom credit volume pricing, a dedicated implementation team, SLA guarantees, and custom security and compliance review. Enterprise is always 'contact us' — there's no list price because the right package depends on your stack, headcount, and use case mix.
