FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not answered here — our team responds within 24 hours.

What does SOC 2 Type I mean, and when is Type II available?+
SOC 2 Type I confirms our security controls are designed correctly — an independent auditor has verified the architecture. Type II requires a 6–12 month observation period. We're in that period now. Contact us for current timeline and interim documentation.
Does ZUUZ store any of our data?+
No. ZUUZ connects via API and reads from your systems at point of request. Nothing is duplicated into a ZUUZ database. Your data stays in your SAP, Salesforce, BambooHR.
What does tamper-evident audit trail mean in practice?+
Every AI action, every agent decision, every data point at the time of a decision is logged immediately and cannot be modified or deleted. If a regulatory review requires evidence of what was known at decision time, the trail provides it — with timestamp, user context, and full data scope.
How is RBAC in ZUUZ different from standard RBAC?+
In most tools, RBAC controls which tabs a user can access. In ZUUZ, RBAC changes what the platform looks like entirely — sidebar, modules, agents, KPIs, and visible data all change by role. CEO and paralegal log into the same platform and have completely different experiences.
What happens to our data if we stop using ZUUZ?+
Nothing — because it was never in ZUUZ. Your SAP, Salesforce, and all other systems remain exactly as they are. ZUUZ's API connections are deactivated. Audit trail logs can be exported before deactivation.
Can ZUUZ support our compliance audit process?+
Yes. The one-click Compliance Audit runner surfaces expired contracts, unsigned docs, Legal Hold status, and access anomalies. The full audit trail is exportable. We can schedule a session with your compliance team.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates, module-by-module costs, and credit pack pricing on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
