FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not answered here — our team responds within 24 hours.

Does the AI make the decision?+
No — always the human. ZUUZ assembles context and delivers a recommendation with named evidence. The decision-maker reviews it and decides whether to agree. Decision support, not decision automation.
What systems does the CEO Cockpit pull from?+
By default: ERP (AR, financial exposure), CRM (deal history), Support (open tickets), Project Mgmt (delivery status), Email (90-day sentiment), Calendar (upcoming interactions). Configured per your connected systems during deployment.
How long does context assembly take?+
Typically under 10 seconds from when the approval is opened. All connected APIs are read simultaneously, not sequentially.
Can it be used for approvals beyond deals?+
Yes — leave requests (Eagle-Eye Review), procurement decisions, compliance reviews. All four surfaces use the same cross-system assembly, configured for the relevant data per approval type.
What if a data source is unavailable?+
The AI surfaces what's available and flags what's missing. The decision is never blocked — the decision-maker is informed of the gap.
Is the audit trail separate from the decision log?+
They're connected. The trail records every data point available at decision time, the AI recommendation with evidence, and the human decision outcome — useful for regulatory reviews and internal governance.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates, module-by-module costs, and credit pack pricing on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
