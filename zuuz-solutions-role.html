FAQ

Common questions.

Anything else — our team responds within 24 hours.

Does every employee get access on day one?+
Yes. Once ZUUZ is deployed, access is role-based — assign a role and the person sees their appropriate view immediately. There is no per-seat commercial negotiation when you add a new hire.
How is role assignment managed?+
Role assignment is managed through the ZUUZ admin console. IT or HR can assign and change roles. Changes take effect immediately — no redeployment or commercial change required.
Can we have custom roles beyond the 11 templates?+
Enterprise plans include custom role configuration. The 11 templates cover the vast majority of enterprise use cases. Custom roles are scoped during implementation for deployments with unusual org structures.
Does adding more users affect pricing?+
No. Pricing is based on Platform Foundation, Department Modules, and Work Credits consumed — not on user or role count. Adding a new employee to an existing role costs nothing additionally.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Three layers: Platform Foundation (fixed monthly), Department Modules (flat fee per department), and Work Credits (charged only on successful task completion). No per-seat pricing. See full pricing.
