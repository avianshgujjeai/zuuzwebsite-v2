FAQ

Common questions.

Will ZUUZ affect our SAP or Salesforce data?+
ZUUZ reads from your systems via API and writes back only on approved transactions — PO generation, ERP updates. It never modifies data without an explicit human-approved trigger. Your systems of record remain the authoritative source.
Do we need to reconfigure our ERP to work with ZUUZ?+
No. ZUUZ connects to your ERP's existing API layer. No configuration changes to SAP, Oracle, or Dynamics are required. The integration reads from standard API endpoints and writes back to standard data models.
What if our ERP is being upgraded or migrated?+
We recommend connecting ZUUZ after your ERP migration is stable. ZUUZ works with the live system — if the system is in flux, the API connections would need to be re-established after migration. We can scope a specific go-live date around your migration timeline.
Does ZUUZ replace Salesforce for our CRM?+
No. Salesforce remains your CRM — the authoritative record for leads, contacts, and deals. ZUUZ reads from Salesforce to provide AI-assembled account context (Sales Co-Pilot) and writes updates back when approved. It makes your Salesforce data more useful at point of need.
