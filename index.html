FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not here — our team responds within 24 hours.

What exactly does ZUUZ do? +
ZUUZ is the AI Operating System for your enterprise. It sits above the tools you already run — SAP, Salesforce, Teams, BambooHR — and runs AI agents across every department. Those agents execute real work: reading contracts, scoring risk, assembling deal context, resolving IT incidents, automating purchase orders. You manage the exceptions. The agents handle the rest.
Do we have to replace any of our existing systems? +
Nothing gets replaced. Your SAP stays. Your Salesforce stays. Teams, Google Workspace, BambooHR — all stay. ZUUZ connects to them via native API and sits above them as an intelligence layer. Disconnect ZUUZ and every system is exactly as it was. No migration, no data loss, no dependency.
How is ZUUZ different from Microsoft Copilot or ChatGPT? +
Copilot and ChatGPT help people do tasks faster — you prompt them, they assist. ZUUZ agents do the tasks without being prompted. A contract received triggers the Contract Risk Review Agent automatically — it reads, flags, scores, and delivers a risk output. No one asked it to. No one waited. The lawyer reviews a scored document, not a raw one. That's not faster assistance. That's a removed task.
Who in our organisation uses ZUUZ? +
ZUUZ supports 11 distinct role personas — from CEO to paralegal. Each role gets a different sidebar, different agents, different KPIs, and different data scope within the same platform. The CEO sees the Approval Cockpit and org-wide sentiment. The paralegal sees Legal DMS and contract workflows. Same platform, same infrastructure, completely different experience per role.
How long does it take to get started? +
Days 1–5: integrations connected — IT reviews our API documentation and approves the connections. Week 2: first agents running on your live data. Week 3–4: first department fully live with measurable time savings. Quarter 1: ROI calculable and reportable to finance. No 18-month implementation. No data migration. Your stack stays where it is.
What's the ROI — how do we justify this to our CFO? +
Every ZUUZ agent shows exactly what it saves — in hours, per task, per person. 6 hours per contract review. 4 hours per PO. The ROI calculator on our pricing page builds the model with your headcount, volume, and hourly rates in 60 seconds. Most enterprise deployments show $3M–$10M in estimated annual value.
How is ZUUZ priced? +
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates and full details on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
Is our data secure? What about compliance? +
ZUUZ is SOC 2 Type I attested. We connect via API only — no data is duplicated into a ZUUZ database. Your enterprise data is never used to train any AI model. Every AI action is logged in a tamper-evident audit trail that's permanent and immutable. Legal Hold is enforced at the system level — not a procedure. Role-based access controls mean each persona sees only what they're authorised to see.

FAQ

Common questions.

Anything else — our team responds within 24 hours.

How quickly can ZUUZ go live?+
For most enterprise environments, the timeline from IT API approval to first agents live on real data is 2–3 weeks. Week 1: security review and API approvals. Week 2: integration configuration and agent setup. Week 3: agents running on live data with measurable time savings. Your stack stays exactly where it is — no migration, no data movement.
Do I have to deploy the entire platform at once?+
No. The recommended path is one department, one use case. The platform infrastructure (integrations, RBAC, security) is deployed once. The first use case runs on that infrastructure. Adding a second department uses the same integrations — days, not months.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Three layers: Platform Foundation (fixed monthly), Department Modules (flat fee per activated department), and Work Credits (charged only on successful task completion — never for failed or incomplete tasks). No per-seat pricing. Ever. See our full pricing page.
Is this replacing our existing tools?+
No. ZUUZ connects to your existing stack — SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, Teams, BambooHR, Zendesk — via API. Your tools stay where they are. ZUUZ adds an AI intelligence and automation layer on top. No rip-and-replace. No data migration.
What does IT have to do?+
IT reviews our SOC 2 documentation and architecture diagrams, then approves the API connection credentials for each connected system. They approve once — they're not involved in ongoing operation or when new use cases are added using the same connections.
What if the AI makes a mistake?+
ZUUZ agents act on defined, structured outputs — they do not make judgment calls in ambiguous situations. Every agent action is logged in the A2A audit trail. High-stakes decisions (like deal approvals) surface AI-assembled context to the human decision-maker — the AI informs, the human decides.
