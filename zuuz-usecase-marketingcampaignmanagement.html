FAQ

Common questions.

Which ad platforms are natively connected?+
Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, Google Ads, and Meta Ads Manager natively. Additional platform connections — LinkedIn Ads, TikTok, programmatic DSPs — are available for enterprise deployments.
How does attribution work for offline conversions?+
If offline conversion data is available in your CRM, the Attribution Agent maps it to the campaign touchpoints that preceded it. For offline events (trade shows, in-store), manual tagging is available.
Can the Social Listening agent track competitor keywords?+
Yes. Competitor brand names, product terms, and campaign hashtags can be tracked alongside your own. Sentiment is scored per brand.
Platform & General
How long does ZUUZ take to go live?+
Under 2 weeks for the first department. Week 1: security review and API credentials. Week 2: integrations connected, agents configured. Week 3: first agents live with time savings your finance team can measure and report.
Does ZUUZ replace our ERP, CRM, or existing tools?+
No. ZUUZ sits on top of your existing stack — SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, Microsoft Teams — and connects via API. No migration. No rip-and-replace. Your current investments stay exactly where they are.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Not per seat. ZUUZ prices by deployment scope and connected systems — not headcount. A 5,000-person organization doesn't pay 5× what a 1,000-person org pays. Pricing details are on the pricing page or shared in the first conversation.
Is our organizational data used to train AI models?+
Never. Your data is never used for AI training. All processing is tenant-isolated. ZUUZ is SOC 2 Type II certified, and the full security architecture is available for your IT team to review in week 1.
Can we pilot with one department before rolling out org-wide?+
Yes — and that is the recommended approach. One department, one broken workflow, measurable time savings by week 3. The same infrastructure then activates for additional departments in days, not months.
What AI models power the platform?+
ZUUZ runs on a combination of proprietary ZUUZ AI and Google Gemini, with real-time search built in. The AI layer is model-agnostic — as better foundation models emerge, the platform updates without requiring any changes from your team.
How does ZUUZ handle role-based access and data security?+
Access controls mirror your org structure. Every AI agent surfaces only the data the requesting user has permission to see — DMS permissions, HR records, financial data — all respected. Full tamper-evident audit trail on every agent action. SOC 2 Type II certified.
