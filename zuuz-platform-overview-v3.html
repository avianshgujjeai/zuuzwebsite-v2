FAQ

Common questions.

Anything else — our team responds within 24 hours.

What makes ZUUZ an AI OS rather than another AI tool?+
An AI OS is infrastructure — it sits on top of your existing stack, connects everything, and provides AI agents with cross-system context. ZUUZ connects to 45+ enterprise systems once and makes that context available to every agent across every department. You don't deploy a new tool per use case — you deploy one platform and activate use cases as you need them.
How is this different from Microsoft Copilot?+
Copilot assists — you prompt it, it responds, you do the work. ZUUZ agents execute — when an approval arrives, the Cockpit assembles context automatically. When an ERP sync fails, A2A detects and resolves it without a human trigger. Different model: assistant vs agentic executor.
How long does implementation take?+
2–3 weeks from IT API approval to first agents live on real data. The platform infrastructure is deployed once. Adding a second department uses the same infrastructure — days, not months.
Is there per-seat pricing?+
No. Every employee gets access. Role assignment determines what they see. The CEO and the paralegal both log into ZUUZ — neither costs more because there are more people.
What happens to our existing systems?+
They stay exactly where they are. ZUUZ connects via API. No migration. No rip-and-replace. Your SAP, Salesforce, Teams, and BambooHR instances are untouched.
