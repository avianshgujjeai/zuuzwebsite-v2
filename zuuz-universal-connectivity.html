FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not answered here — our team responds within 24 hours.

How long does a connection take?+
For systems on our pre-built list, typically 1–3 business days once IT has approved the API credentials. The technical work is hours — the time is in the review and approval process.
What does IT actually have to do?+
Review our API documentation for each integration, approve the credentials, and confirm the connection is live. No custom build, no middleware configuration, no ongoing maintenance.
Does ZUUZ store any of our data?+
No. ZUUZ connects via API and reads from your systems at point of request. Nothing is duplicated into a ZUUZ database. Disconnect ZUUZ — your systems are exactly as they were.
What if we have a system not on the 45+ list?+
We'll tell you directly. If a system isn't currently supported, we give an honest timeline or discuss a custom integration engagement. We don't over-promise.
Can ZUUZ write back to our systems?+
Both read and write. ZUUZ reads to assemble context and writes back where needed — updating leave status in BambooHR after approval, creating a PO in SAP, updating contract status in DMS. Scope configured per integration.
How does ZUUZ handle API rate limits?+
ZUUZ manages rate limits automatically using a queuing system. A2A also monitors rate limit events as a system health indicator.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates, module-by-module costs, and credit pack pricing on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
