FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not answered here — our team responds within 24 hours.

Do we need IT to build a workflow?+
IT is involved once — to approve the API connections. After that, operations, HR, and legal build and modify workflows in the drag-and-drop builder. No IT ticket, no developer required.
How is this different from RPA or Zapier?+
RPA automates fixed steps. Zapier connects apps at the event level. ZUUZ sits on top of 45+ pre-wired enterprise systems and includes AI agents as native steps — a workflow can trigger a risk-scoring agent, receive the output, and route based on the score. That's AI-native, not an API chain.
Can workflows trigger agents and vice versa?+
Yes — this is a core design principle. Agent output triggers the next step. A workflow step triggers an agent. A contract received → agent reads → workflow routes by risk level → DMS updated → Slack notified. One continuous AI-native process.
What if a workflow fails mid-execution?+
Workflows include TRY/CATCH logic as a native step. You define what happens on failure — retry, escalate, log, or halt. Every execution is logged with status so if something stops, the audit trail shows exactly where and why.
Can we migrate from ServiceNow or Workato?+
We review your existing workflows during implementation. In most cases the ZUUZ equivalent is built fresh in the builder — it's faster than migrating because the integrations are already wired.
How many workflows can run simultaneously?+
No hard cap for enterprise accounts. Workflows are event-driven — they scale with your event volume.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates, module-by-module costs, and credit pack pricing on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
