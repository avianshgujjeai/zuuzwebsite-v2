FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Anything not answered here — our team responds within 24 hours.

How many agents come pre-built?+
The library covers Legal, HR, Procurement, Productivity, Executive, IT, and Marketing — covering the most common high-cost workflows in each department. The library grows as the platform matures. You're not capped at a fixed count.
Do agents work on live data or a knowledge base?+
Live data only. Every agent pulls from your connected systems at the moment it runs — not a cached snapshot. When the Contract Risk Review Agent reads a contract, it's reading the current file right now.
Can we customise what an agent does?+
Agents come pre-configured. The Workflow Builder lets you chain agents together and add conditional logic across departments. Custom agent configuration is available for enterprise accounts.
How do agents handle sensitive data?+
Agents operate within your role-based access controls. An agent running for the HR persona reads only HR-authorised data. ZUUZ connects via API and reads what the authorised role can access — no more, no less.
What if an agent makes a mistake?+
Every Work Credit is logged in the tamper-evident audit trail. Agents deliver outputs for human review, not decisions. The human always decides. The audit trail shows exactly what data the agent had and what it concluded.
How is ZUUZ priced?+
Pricing is structured across three layers — a Platform Foundation fee, Department Module fees (one per team you activate), and Work Credits that only charge when agents complete real tasks. There are no per-seat fees, ever. See current rates, module-by-module costs, and credit pack pricing on our pricing page, or talk to our team — enterprise pricing is always a tailored conversation.
