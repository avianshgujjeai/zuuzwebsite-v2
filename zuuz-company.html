The founding pricing principle
Our founding premise

AI should do the work.
Not assist with it.

The category has a language problem. "AI assistant" and "AI agent" are used interchangeably — but they describe fundamentally different things. An assistant responds when you prompt it. An agent acts when something happens.

We built ZUUZ around the agent model: event-triggered, cross-system, autonomous within defined parameters, and observable via a full audit trail. The workflows didn't change. The humans running them get their time back.

The no-per-seat pricing wasn't a marketing decision. It was a conviction: if AI is genuinely useful, everyone in the company should have access. You shouldn't be choosing who gets it based on seat budget.

Forged by insight.
Driven by precision.
Our tagline is a commitment. Every agent output is grounded in cross-system data. Every time-saving claim is measurable. Every pricing model is transparent.
